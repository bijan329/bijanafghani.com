About The Site

I put this site together in a couple of days, although I am constantly updating and improving it. I built BlackJack, Roulette, and Photo Puzzle with vanilla JavaScript, and I am currently working on adding a Slot Machine interface using JQuery. The most complicated part of building BlackJack was creating an algorithm to automatically attribute a 1 or an 11 to an Ace based on your current total. This was done by summing all player cards that are not Aces. One cannot have more than one Ace worth 11 or else your total will be greater than 21. If the number of Aces in your hand is greater than 1, all Aces except one's value must be 1. Then based on your total after those are accounted for, the last Ace will have a value of 1 if an addition of 11 to your total would cause a bust, otherwise the value will be 11. The most difficult part of Roulette, however, was animating the roulette wheel using CSS. The site itself is run by a simple AWS EC2 Apache web server.
